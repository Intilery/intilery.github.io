Google Tag Manager

To Install the Intilery website tracking tag, follow these steps.

Tag Snippet#

  1. Log in to your Intilery account, navigate to "Tag"

  1. Then click "Add Intilery to your website" to open the tag snippet

  1. Click "Copy Snippet" to copy the tag code to your clipboard.

Google Tag Manager#

  1. Log in to your Google Tag Manager account.
  2. Navigate to your website/container
  3. Click Tags
  4. Click New

  1. Click the pencil icon to select tag type

  1. Click "Custom HTML"

  2. Enter a suitable name in the field "Untitled Tag"

  1. Paste the tag snippet from Intilery into the HTML field (do not change anything)
  2. Click on the pencil icon for the triggering section
  3. Select "All Pages" to add the Intilery tag to all pages of your website
  4. Click Save
  5. Click Submit to submit the changes (added tag) to your website
  6. Submit the changes
